H Street NE Food and Fun

H Street Northeast is one of the coolest neighborhoods in DC. This one-and-a-half mile stretch has a wide variety of restaurants and hangout spots sure to satisfy any craving.

Buredo

Buredo is an innovative sushi spot near 14th street with an eye for fresh, fantastic food. They specialize in burrito-sized sushi rolls, tasty bowls, and super-fresh salads. There are a ton of options for the seasoned sushi fan and for soon-to-be fans. Be sure to check out their super-cool Blade Runner mural while you're there!

My recommendation: Try the Nikkia Bowl for something just a little bit spicy!

Sticky Rice

Sticky Rice is a hip Asian-fusion restaurant with a laid-back atmosphere and plenty of tasty meals. They specialize in rice and noodle dishes with a variety of toppings, and have plenty of tasty options for vegans and vegetarians too! One of their more off-the-wall options is their series of bucketfuls of tater tots (or sweet tater tots!).

My recommendation: The Millennium

Chupacabra

Chupacabra is a Mexican restaurant with tons of personality and flavor. Serving traditional Latin food as fresh as can be, this place makes some of the best tacos, burritos, and anything else Latin you can think of! Most of the dining space is outside, so I highly recommend you stop by in the warmer months.

My recommendation: Corn tortilla tacos al pastor

Solid State Books

Solid State Books is a huge independent bookstore with a wide variety of hand-selected books of all sorts of genres. With friendly and enthusiastic staff and books covering almost every wall, you'll never be stuck not knowing what to read next! They even have a built-in coffee bar and a place to relax with your drink and new reads!

Atlas Performing Arts Center

The Atlas Performing Arts Center is home to performances of all types. They have four separate performance spaces, and host events from groups throughout the DC region. They even perform family-oriented and free events from time to time, so it's always worth checking out what's on their schedule!
