H Street NE Food and Fun

H Street Northeast is one of the coolest neighborhoods in DC. This one-and-a-half mile stretch has a wide variety of restaurants and hangout spots sure to satisfy any craving.

Restaurants

Buredo

Buredo is an innovative sushi spot near 14th street with an eye for fresh, fantastic food. They specialize in burrito-sized sushi rolls, tasty bowls, and super-fresh salads. There are a ton of options for the seasoned sushi fan and for soon-to-be fans. Be sure to check out their super-cool Blade Runner mural while you're there!

My recommendation: Try the Nikkia Bowl for something just a little bit spicy!

 Sticky Rice

Sticky Rice is a hip Asian-fusion restaurant with a laid-back atmosphere and plenty of tasty meals. They specialize in rice and noodle dishes with a variety of toppings, and have plenty of tasty options for vegans and vegetarians too! One of their more off-the-wall options is their series of bucketfuls of tater tots (or sweet tater tots!).

My recommendation: The Millenium

 Chupacabra

You are sure to get the freshest seafood when you visit San Diego. San Diego has many popular seafood restaurants where you can dine on fish caught just a few miles away. You will also find seafood dishes like ceviche and fish tacos at many restaurants and taco shops in San Diego.

Activities & Fun

Food Trucks

If you're still hungry, be on the lookout for a food truck! You're sure to find one at one of San Diego's many outdoor festivals or events, or even parked at a local brewery or mall. Food trucks offer dining on-the-go and serve up tasty gourmet meals. There is a food truck for every appetite: bratwursts, salads, crabcakes, hot dogs, crepes, pierogi, and more.
